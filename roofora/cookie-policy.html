Cookie Policy:

This Cookie Policy explains how Roofora Roofing & Construction Services (“we”, “our”, or “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. Cookies help improve your browsing experience, personalize content, and ensure certain features like service requests, project galleries, and consultation pages function properly.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember user preferences, improve functionality, and analyze site performance. In a construction context, cookies may help remember your project interests, service inquiries, or consultation details.

2. How We Use Cookies:

Roofora uses cookies to enhance your experience while browsing roofing services, construction solutions, and project details. Cookies may help:

  • Remember your preferences, project interests, or preferred service types.
  • Ensure contact forms and service request features work correctly.
  • Improve navigation, performance, and overall website functionality.

3. Analytics & Performance:

We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our website, such as:

  • Pages viewed (e.g., roofing services, construction project galleries).
  • Time spent on service or project detail pages.
  • General website traffic trends.

This information helps improve website performance, user experience, and service offerings.

4. Types of Cookies We May Use:

These may include:

  • Essential Cookies – for core functionality like service requests and navigation.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies – to help us analyze site usage and improve experience.
  • Marketing Cookies – to deliver relevant service offers, promotions, or construction solutions.

Cookies generally do not store sensitive personal information.

5. Third-Party Cookies:

Some third-party services, such as analytics or embedded content providers, may place cookies on your device. These cookies are governed by the policies of the respective third parties.

6. Your Choices:

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect:

  • Online service requests or consultation bookings
  • Display of project galleries and service details
  • Personalized user experience across our website

By continuing to use this website, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy. If you have any questions, please contact us at Roofora@gmail.com
