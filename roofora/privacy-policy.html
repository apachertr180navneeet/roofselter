Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Roofora Roofing & Construction Services collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website. We value your privacy and aim to provide a safe and personalized experience while exploring our roofing solutions, construction services, project guides, and building materials.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you request a quote, schedule a site visit, contact us, subscribe to updates, create an account, or inquire about construction services. This may include your name, email address, phone number, project details, and billing or service-related information.

We also collect limited non-personal information such as browser type, device information, IP address, and general website usage data. This helps us enhance website performance, improve service offerings, and optimize your overall browsing experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process service requests, project inquiries, and consultations efficiently.
  • Respond to questions and provide personalized customer support.
  • Send important updates, confirmations, or service-related offers (if opted in).

Non-personal data helps us analyze website traffic, maintain security, prevent unauthorized activity, and ensure smooth website functionality. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

  • Information is used to manage service requests, client accounts, and project communications.
  • Website usage data helps improve performance, navigation, and user experience.
  • Security measures are implemented to protect user data and maintain safe operations.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Sharing of Information

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers such as material suppliers, contractors, payment processors, or technical support providers when necessary to operate our website and deliver services.

Information may also be disclosed if required by law, to protect our legal rights, or to ensure safety within our construction operations.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve functionality, analyze performance, and personalize your browsing experience. Cookies help remember project preferences, service interests, and previous interactions. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services, website features, or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Roofora@gmail.com
